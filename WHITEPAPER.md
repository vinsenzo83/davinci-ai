## 1. Executive Summary

**DaVinci AI** is a next-generation Web3 creative intelligence platform inspired by the polymathic genius of Leonardo da Vinci. Built natively on **BNB Chain**, DaVinci AI unifies AI-powered art generation, gamified airdrop campaigns, and NFT ownership into a single cohesive ecosystem governed by the **$DAVINCI** utility token.

The platform addresses a critical gap in the Web3 creative economy: the lack of an accessible, rewarding, and community-governed environment where digital artists, gamers, and crypto enthusiasts can create, earn, and own their digital works.

**Key Highlights:**
- Total Supply: **8,888,888,888 $DAVINCI** (BEP-20)
- Chain: **BNB Smart Chain**
- TGE: **Q4 2025**
- Core Products: Game Hub, AI Atelier, NFT Marketplace, DAO Governance
- AI Partners: OpenAI, Midjourney, KlingAI, HeyGen, Wrtn.AI, Nouvo.AI

In 2024, the AI art market generated over **$3.2 billion** in value, while NFT trading volume on BNB Chain exceeded **$800 million**. Yet, these ecosystems remain largely fragmented.

DaVinci AI was founded on the principle that Leonardo da Vinci embodied — the seamless fusion of science, art, and human ingenuity. Just as da Vinci crossed the boundaries between painting, engineering, and anatomy, DaVinci AI crosses the boundary between AI creativity, blockchain ownership, and community gaming.

We believe the future of digital art is:
- **AI-Assisted** — removing technical barriers to artistic expression
- **Community-Owned** — governed and rewarded through decentralized mechanisms
- **Gamified** — making participation engaging and fun
- **Permanently Owned** — secured on-chain as immutable NFTs

## 5. Platform Architecture

### 5.1 DaVinci Game Hub

The **DaVinci Game Hub** is a Telegram-native and WebApp gaming platform that drives community engagement through skill-based mini-games and competitive leaderboards.

**Features:**
- **Mini-Games**: Pixel Art Builder, AI Guess Challenge, NFT Roulette, Da Vinci Puzzle
- **Creative Credits System**: In-platform currency earned through gameplay
- **Season System**: Quarterly seasons with tiered airdrop rewards
- **Rank Tiers**: Bronze → Silver → Gold → Platinum → Da Vinci

**Season Mechanics:**
```
Season Duration:    12 weeks
Airdrop Pool:       5% of season allocation
Top 10% earners:    60% of airdrop pool
Top 10-30%:         30% of airdrop pool
Top 30-50%:         10% of airdrop pool
```

**Technical Stack:**
- Frontend: React / Telegram WebApp SDK
- Backend: Node.js + BNB Chain smart contract integration
- Randomness: Chainlink VRF (verifiable randomness)

### 5.3 NFT Renaissance

The **NFT Renaissance** module enables seamless minting, trading, and showcasing of AI-generated artworks as permanent on-chain assets.

**Smart Contract Standards:**
- Token Standard: **BEP-721** (unique NFTs) and **BEP-1155** (editions)
- Metadata: **IPFS** (InterPlanetary File System) for permanent storage
- Royalties: **EIP-2981** royalty standard (creator-set, 0–15%)

**Marketplace Features:**
- Fixed price listings
- Timed auctions (English & Dutch)
- Bundle sales
- Collection creation and management
- Trait rarity scoring

**Fee Structure:**
```
Platform Fee:         2% of sale price
Creator Royalty:      Configurable (0–15%)
DAO Treasury:         25% of platform fees
Burn Mechanism:       10% of platform fees burned
```

**NFT Provenance:**
Every NFT includes on-chain metadata linking to:
- Original AI generation parameters
- Creator wallet address
- Generation timestamp
- AI tool used
- IPFS content hash

## Appendix B — Glossary

| Term | Definition |
